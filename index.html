MSc student in GIS & Geospatial Engineering

University of Tehran · School of Surveying and Geospatial Engineering

I am a master's student in Geographic Information Science at the University of Tehran, where I also completed my BSc in Surveying and Geospatial Engineering. My research sits at the intersection of GeoAI, spatial optimization, and environmental monitoring — using deep learning and geospatial analysis to address real-world problems in disaster response, land cover change, and natural resource management.

My current work focuses on developing Spatial Decision Support Systems for post-earthquake humanitarian logistics, combining GIS with metaheuristic optimization. I have also applied transformer-based architectures and convolutional models to satellite imagery for wetland mapping, water body segmentation, and landslide susceptibility assessment. Beyond research, I build interactive WebGIS applications using Leaflet, CesiumJS, and PostGIS to make geospatial data accessible to wider audiences.
