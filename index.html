University of Tehran · School of Surveying and Geospatial Engineering

I am a master's student in Geographic Information Science at the University of Tehran, where I also completed my BSc in Surveying and Geospatial Engineering. My research sits at the intersection of GeoAI, spatial optimization, and environmental monitoring using deep learning and geospatial analysis to address real-world problems in disaster response, land cover change, and natural resource management.

Outside of research, I am a creative person who enjoys different forms of art and expression. I draw portraits, love story-driven video games with rich character development and immersive narratives, and enjoy watching movies. I have a curious mind that likes to find connections and patterns among seemingly unrelated things, a habit that, I think, also shapes how I approach geospatial problems.

I am currently looking for graduate positions where I can pursue my passion for GIScience and GeoAI, learn from and collaborate with researchers and experts in the field, and apply my knowledge and skills to solve real-world problems that make a positive impact. My CV page has more details on my background, and my projects page covers what I have been working on.
